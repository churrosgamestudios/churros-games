# Privacy Policy for Gravitten VR

**Effective Date:** July 2026  
**Developer:** Churros Game Studios 

### 1. Introduction
Welcome to our Privacy Policy. **Gravitten VR** is a fully offline, single-player VR game developed by **Churros Game Studios**.

We respect your privacy. Because our game operates entirely locally on your device, our policy is very straightforward: we do not collect your personal data.

### 2. Data Collection and Storage
**Gravitten VR** and by extension the Developer (Churros Game Studios) will **NOT** collect or store any personally identifiable information on any server belonging to the Developer for any period of time. 

Personally identifiable information includes a user's real name, location, photos, usage traits, or information that could otherwise be used to ascertain a user's physical identity. The app, nor the Developer, will ever sell, release, redistribute, or otherwise transmit a user's personal data to any third-party for any reason. 

All user progress and game settings are handled and saved strictly locally on your device.

### 3. VR Hardware and Gameplay Data
The app does not store any unique device information that could lead to the personal identification of the user. However, the app handles generalized information about the device - such as button clicks, headset positioning, and controller orientation strictly locally in real-time, as this is inherently required for the VR app to function. This data is never recorded, transmitted, or stored.

### 4. Distribution Platform (Meta Quest Ecosystem)
Please note that the policies set forth in this Privacy Policy apply only to the app itself and may not reflect those of the Distribution Platform (the Meta Quest ecosystem) where the app is hosted. 

Meta Platforms, Inc. may process your account and device information to enable core platform services. The Developer may utilize aggregated information about the app's usage (such as total download numbers or crash statistics) as provided by the Distributing Platform for the purpose of bettering the app, patching bugs, and further categorization of the product. Please refer to Meta's Privacy Policy to learn about how your platform data is managed.

### 5. Direct User Feedback
Information provided directly to the Developer by the User (for example, via email regarding bugs, support requests, complaints, or feedback about the app) may be stored for support and legal reasons, and is handled strictly within the guidelines of applicable law. You may request the deletion of this correspondence at any time.

### 6. Children's Privacy
Because our game does not collect any personal information from any user through normal gameplay, we inherently do not knowingly collect or store any personal data from children.
